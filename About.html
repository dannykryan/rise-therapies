About Me

Hi, I'm Gemma!

As a registered midwife and mother, I understand the joys and challenges of pregnancy firsthand. I am passionate about providing expecting mothers with personalized, compassionate care that promotes a positive pregnancy experience.

I have a first-class honours degree in midwifery and have been trained in complementary therapies by Denise Tiran, one of the leading authorities in the field. I know that pregnancy can be a stressful time, and I am dedicated to offering services that provide relaxation, self-care and support that are often not provided by the NHS.

I believe that every expecting mother deserves to have someone who truly listens to them and understand their needs and desires. I am here to hold your hand and support you throughout your pregnancy journey and make it as comfortable and enjoyable as possible.

If you think you could benefit from my services, please reach out to me on 0703095582 or by completing my contact form and I'll aim to get back to you within 24 hours.
