Hi, I'm Gemma!

I am a registered midwife and mother to two beautiful boys. I work as an independent specialist midwife therapist and as an NHS birth centre midwife. I am passionate about promoting a positive pregnancy experience through a holistic approach, nurturing mind, body, and soul. Stimulating the mind through education to empower informed decision-making, nourishing the body with a combination of complimentary therapies tailored to the individual and changing needs and nourishing the soul with time to talk, reflect and discuss concerns and offer practical, realistic, and balanced advice. I seek to provide women and their families holistic, compassionate care catered to each family’s unique needs and desires.

At each appointment I use a combination of complementary therapies designed to treat your individual and changing symptoms and reduce the effects of common problems experienced at different stages of pregnancy. I want you to feel cared for and pampered, nurtured and empowered throughout your pregnancy and postnatal period, with a focus on improving your health and wellbeing, treating you holistically and ensuring you have a positive experience without the time restraints and the limited resources of conventional care. You will have time to discuss any pregnancy related symptoms or concerns you may be experiencing and be offered a combination of complimentary therapies at each appointment; this therapeutic clinical intervention will trigger the body’s own healing capacity and help the body adjust to the changes of pregnancy.

There is a huge demand for complimentary therapies in pregnancy, estimated over 80% of pregnant women use complimentary therapies for relaxation and stress relief. Increasingly women are seeking natural ways of dealing with specific issues such as sickness, backache, and avoiding induction of labour. Women want some ‘me time’ and a ‘listening ear’ seeking services that give an aspect of care often not provided by an overstretched NHS. I undertook additional training in complimentary therapies with Denise Tiran of Expectancy. Denise pioneered complimentary therapies as a specialist field within midwifery education and practice in the early 1980s and is acknowledged as one of the leading authorities in the field.

One of the many reasons I became a midwife was for job satisfaction, I want to end each day proud of the service I deliver, honouring the women I work with by providing them with a uniquely holistic approach. Midwife means ‘with woman’ and I want a fulfilling and empowering experience for myself and the women I have the privilege to share this incredible journey with, I will offer a calm and reassuring presence in which to build a trusting relationship, whether I see you for a one-off relaxation treatment or care for you throughout the entirety of your journey with our bespoke therapies and private midwife package.
