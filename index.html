Post-Date Package - £250

Three tailored treatment sessions designed to help you avoid medical induction and induce labour naturally.

If you have gone past your due date, my Post-Date Package aims to encourage the natural onset of labor and avoid the need for a medical induction. Each 90 minute long appointment includes 60 minutes of hands-on treatment and will use a combination of techniques including acupressure, reflexology, structural reflex zone therapy, aromatherapy, and massage to make you feel relaxed, calm, and release endorphins and oxytocin, while reducing the stress hormones cortisol and adrenaline.

These techniques are safe for you and your baby and aim to initiate or enhance contractions, promote cervical dilation, and aid decent of the fetus into the pelvis. The advantages are if labor does not commence, midwives report a more favorable cervix and increased chance of experiencing minimal intervention.

During the appointments, we will teach you and your birth partner how to stimulate acupressure points so that you can continue this treatment at home. You will also receive an aromatherapy oil blend of uterotonic oils (nature’s syntocinon) to take home and continue treatment.
