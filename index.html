Birth-Preparation Treatments

What is birth preparation treatments? (from 37 weeks)

Included in this series of treatments is time to talk with me an independent midwife, who will give you honest and evidence based advise. We can discuss your worries, debrief from a previous birth, discuss your birth plan, plan for an active birth, talk over pain relief options in labour or what to expect after a caesarean section and how to care for your new-born. Our time will be tailored to your needs.

At each appointment you will receive a complementary therapy treatment to help you feel relaxed, empowered and pampered. Reducing the stress and worries one feels before going into labour, ensuring you feel relaxed will increase the production of oxytocin and decrease cortisol and make it more likely that you will go into labour spontaneously.

£375

Complementary Therapy - Post-Date Treatments

What is the Complementary Therapy Post-date treatment? (40+ wk)

I offer post-date treatments to encourage the natural onset of labour and avoid the need for a medical induction of labour. These treatments offer a potential opportunity reduce the medicalisation of your childbirth, increase the chance of you progressing through a medical induction with minimal intervention supporting physiological birth.

The optimal time for this treatment is round 40 weeks, however you may have an induction of labour you are trying to avoid booked before 40 weeks.

£250
